SpeedRead - Accelerate Your Reading

Welcome to SpeedRead, your go-to web application for enhancing your reading speed and comprehension. Whether you're a student, professional, or avid reader, SpeedRead is designed to help you read faster and retain more information.

Features

  • Adjustable Speed: Customize the reading speed to match your comfort level and gradually increase it as you improve.
  • Word-by-Word Display: Text is shown one word at a time using Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP), reducing eye movement and improving focus.
  • Text Input: Paste text from any source — articles, documents, or eBooks.
  • Stay Focused: A clean, distraction-free interface keeps your attention on the text.
  • Multi-Device Support: Access SpeedRead from your computer, tablet, or smartphone.
