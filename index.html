SpeedRead - Accelerate Your Reading

Welcome to SpeedRead, your go-to web application for enhancing your reading speed and comprehension. Whether you're a student, professional, or avid reader, SpeedRead is designed to help you read faster and retain more information.

Features

  • Adjustable Speed: Customize the reading speed to match your comfort level and gradually increase it as you improve.
  • Chunking Technique: Uses the latest research in cognitive psychology to break down text into manageable chunks for faster reading.
  • Text Input: Easily paste or upload text from various sources, including articles, documents, and eBooks.
  • Stay Focused: Minimize distractions with a clean, user-friendly interface that helps you concentrate on the text.
  • Multi-Device Support: Access SpeedRead from any device, whether it's your computer, tablet, or smartphone.
